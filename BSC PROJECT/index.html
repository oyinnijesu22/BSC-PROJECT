Welcome to the E-Voting Platform

Secure, fast, and easy online voting for our department elections.

Login to Vote

The E-Voting platform is a secure, easy-to-use system designed to allow students of the Department of Computer Science to cast their votes online. This eliminates the need for physical ballot boxes and ensures transparency, fairness, and quick counting of votes.

Our mission is to create a democratic environment where every student can exercise their voting rights from anywhere, using a device with internet access.
